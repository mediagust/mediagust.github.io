15 Digital Marketing Trends of 2020

Digital marketing is an ever-changing industry. Every month, new changes appear in the world of social networks, search (Google) or content marketing. New technologies are coming into our lives, like artificial intelligence. Algorithms are more efficient and intelligent. All these advances make digital marketing ever more accurate, effective and difficult to understand.

How will digital marketing evolve in the coming years? What are the trends in digital marketing in 2020? You're probably asking yourself these 2 questions. And this is understandable.

Digital marketing is an ever-changing industry. Every month, new changes appear in the world of social networks, search (Google) or content marketing. New technologies are coming into our lives, like artificial intelligence. Algorithms are more efficient and intelligent. All these advances make digital marketing ever more accurate, effective and difficult to understand.

The key to taming marketing in the digital age is to stay abreast of new trends to understand how you will guide your strategy today and, in the years, to come. Indeed, what worked for you before may not work now because once again digital marketing is an ever-changing industry.

After doing a few hours of research to help you stay up to date on the news, I concocted an article covering the 15 trends in digital marketing that you should not ignore in 2020 (and beyond).

You will discover following 15 digital marketing trends of 2020:

  • 1. Microcontent takes other forms
  • 2. The social commercial will continue its expansion
  • 3. Pinterest and LinkedIn (re)win the heart of marketers
  • 4. User generated content (UGC)
  • 5. Social network television
  • 6. Podcasts
  • 7. Micro-influencers will be more sought after
  • 8. Will influencers post more or less?
  • 9. Advertising automation is progressing
  • 10. The cost of social ads may increase
  • 11. Search results are changing ... and your organic traffic may decrease
  • 12. More importance to optimization for voice SEO
  • 13. Artificial intelligence
  • 14. Interactive content (again and again)
  • 15. The number of touch points before conversion will increase

Let's start by talking about top trends of the year 2020. That's probably what you're most interested in (you may follow the link to get detailed information on each topic). Most of the trends mentioned in this article are already adopted by many major brands. I am thinking in particular of:

  • Instagram Shopping
  • Advertising Automation
  • The Chatbots
  • Interactive Content
  • Partnerships with Micro-Influencers

This is not the case for all SMEs that still have a long way to go. If you are one, you have noticed that using micro-influencers is a strategy to test this year (because it is inexpensive). Going live regularly (simultaneously) on social networks can also help you interact with your community.

For social networks, remember that commitment remains the most important factor for your success, regardless of the platform. What is certain is that in the coming years, one should expect to see artificial intelligence invade our world with increasingly intelligent robots, the adoption of voice assistants like Alexa, etc.

I also put a small piece on LinkedIn and podcasts! I think, in the coming years, they will become more important for marketers. I hope this article helped you see more clearly about digital marketing trends! Internet, SEO and content marketing are essential pillars of digital marketing. The combination of these strategies is the best way to start and develop a profitable business.
