Here are some common questions and their answers, you may have during order your first press release with us. If your question is not listed here, please contact us...

1. Do You Publish Non English Press Releases?

No, we only take up press releases in the English language only as it fits our standard domestic (US) options. We don't allow contents that are not in English.

2. What Forms of Payment Does Media Gust Accept?

We accept all major debit and credit cards as Visa, MC, Amex and Discover for press release distribution services through our secure and encrypted online ordering system.

3. How quickly can a press release be distributed?

By default, press releases are distributed within 24 hours Monday to Friday. If you have special requirements for your press release to be distributed on a specific day or time, please make a note in the "SPECIAL INSTRUCTIONS" field of the order form.

4. When my press release will be available in search engines and Google News?

Generally, 2-3 days after distribution. Your press release will be available and indexed in all major search engines and will be listed in Google News as well.
