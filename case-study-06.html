Electrica.

  • Projects
  • Case Study

Summary.

Electrica LLC, is the leading electrical equipment manufacturer and service provider in the United States. Despite their leadership in their industry, Electrica felt as though their website was not performing as well as it could be as the company's chief marketing engine. They turned to MediaGust for help.

The Strategy.

We re-designed their website and developed a multi-channel strategy that included content creation, graphics, guest post and mail marketing. Local searches remain a key focal point of Google, so we knew it was important to implement some proven strategies for quick wins.

The Result.

Through a careful blend of web design, SEO, reputation management and paid media campaigns, we delivered on our promises, including:

  • YEARLY GROWTH IN WEBSITE TRAFFIC - 262%                    INCREASE IN ONLINE SALES - 103%
  • INCREASE IN ORGANIC TRAFFIC - 112%                    INCREASE IN SOCIAL TRAFFIC - 247%
